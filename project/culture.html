Festivals and Traditional Foods of Tarlac

Festivals

  • Malatarlak Festival – Celebrates the resiliency and creativity of the people of Tarlac.
  • Kaisa Festival – Showcases the unity of multicultural communities in the province.

Traditional Foods

  • Chicharon Camiling – Crispy pork rind, a popular delicacy.
  • Iniruban – Green rice cake made from glutinous rice.
